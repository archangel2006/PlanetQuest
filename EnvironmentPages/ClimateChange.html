Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. While some changes are natural, modern warming is driven mainly by human activities — especially burning fossil fuels and deforestation.

Why it matters

  • Melting ice and rising sea levels threaten coastal communities.
  • Extreme weather events (storms, heatwaves, droughts) are increasing.
  • Shifts in habitats and food supply affect biodiversity and livelihoods.

What you can do

  • Reduce energy use: switch off unused lights, choose efficient appliances.
  • Travel smart: walk, cycle, carpool, or use public transport where possible.
  • Support renewable energy and sustainable policies in your community.
